Product Description

LinkedMail AI is a Chrome browser extension that reads publicly visible LinkedIn profile information and uses AI to generate personalized cold emails. It is designed for sales professionals, recruiters, and founders who reach out to prospects on LinkedIn.

Pricing

  • Free Plan: 5 emails per month at no cost.
  • Pro Plan: $19/month — unlimited emails per month. Billed monthly. Cancel anytime.

Subscription & Billing
