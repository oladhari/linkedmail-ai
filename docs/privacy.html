Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

LinkedMail AI ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Account data: Your email address and hashed password when you register.
  • Usage data: How many emails you have generated this month (to enforce free plan limits).
  • Payment data: Handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

What we do NOT collect

  • LinkedIn profile data is sent to our server only to generate your email and is never stored or logged.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing.
  • We do not use tracking pixels or third-party analytics.

Third-party services

  • OpenAI: Profile text is sent to OpenAI's API to generate email content. Subject to OpenAI's privacy policy.
  • Stripe: Payment processing. Subject to Stripe's privacy policy.

Data retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account exists. You can request deletion by emailing us.

Contact

Questions? Email support@linkedmailai.com
